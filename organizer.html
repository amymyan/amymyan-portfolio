music library — two ways photos get in:
1. local folder — copy/download files into assets/music/ on your computer, then click refresh library or sync from folder
2. R2 only — upload to Cloudflare R2, then click register R2 filename and enter the exact filename (refresh alone cannot scan R2)
select photos in the library (shift-click for range), then create or add to a contact sheet. on the board: shift-click or cmd-click sheets to select multiple, drag a box on empty space, then drag any selected sheet to move them together. click the × on a photo to remove it from the sheet (it stays in the library).
from left / from top = position on the page (% of board width) — or just drag the sheet.

adding media: upload files to R2 in assets/<page>/, then click register R2 file to add them to the page. Or add locally via sync from folder only runs when you click it — removing a photo (×) keeps it off the page even if the file is still on disk or R2.
drag to arrange, size slider on top of each photo, pink rotation dot above photo to spin. click a caption to edit — it shows centered under the video on the live site.
undo button or ⌘Z (ctrl+Z on windows) reverses your last change on this page.
video thumbnails: click upload thumb, pick an image, then upload that same file to R2 in assets/<page>/ — one upload, R2 only.
